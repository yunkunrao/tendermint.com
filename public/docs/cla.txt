To send a signed agreement to the Tendermint project,
please send an email with the following information:

* Your full name
* Your title, if signing a corporate CLA
* Your Github username, if signing an individual CLA
* The version and type (corporate or individual) of the CLA you are agreeing to

Please send the email to cla@tendermint.com.


If you do not own the contribution, please send an
email to cla@tendermint.com explaining who owns it.

Thanks!
